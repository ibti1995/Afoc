Marhaba

Erth's storied past and rare blend of world-class 5-star services stands unmatched in Abu Dhabi. Formerly known as Armed Forces Officers Club and Hotel, the property was built in 1997 to honour the legacy of HH Sheikh Zayed bin Sultan Al Nahyan, and catered to members of the UAE's Armed Forces and their families. Since opening to the public in 2003, Erth, beckons its discerning guests to live its story in premier business and leisure settings.

Always Honorable

Our roots - serving the Armed Forces - establishes the foundations of how we act: principled, reliable, and trustworthy

Proudly Emirati

Proud of our culture and heritage, our commitment to providing guests with unique memorable experiences reflects the warmth and welcome of genuine Emirati hospitality.

Genuinely Personal

Our dedicated team of associates strive hard to ensure every moment of the guest's journey is especially delightful.

Stay with a Story

A 15 minutes' drive from downtown Abu Dhabi, Erth lies in close proximity to the iconic Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque and the Wahat Al Karama war memorial. Offering guests an evocative gateway into the past, present and future of the UAE, Erth's every detail speaks of rich Emirati cultural heritage and the property's own military history. From the iconic falcon-inspired architecture, designed by esteemed French architect, Roger Taillibert, in the mid-1980's, to the precise service provided to our guests, every stay at Erth is a story and every story is unique.
